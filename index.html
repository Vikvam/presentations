Motivation

  • Flagship NLP systems (like GPT-3) require little to no dataset specific training data.
  • These results suggest that the supervision to modern pre-training methods within web-scale collections of text surpasses that of high-quality crowd-labeled NLP datasets.

Previous Work

  1. Weakly-supervised pre-training
    • Static softmax classifier for less than 2000 classes.
  2. VirTex, ConVIRT - transformer-based language modeling, masked language modeling, and contrastive objectives to learn image representations from text.

Dataset

  1. MS-COCO + Visual Genome + YFCC100M
    • Filtered to images with titles and/or descriptions in English
    • 15 million photos
  2. WIT (WebImageText)
    • From a variety of publicly available sources on the Internet
    • 400 million (image, text) pairs
